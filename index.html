Our story

Molly & Me Pecans started in 2008 when Kay Holseberg started picking up pecans at her family's farm, trying different recipes, and sharing them with family and friends. Today Kay is still making her farm fresh, all-natural, gluten-free pecans on her own farm and sharing them with everyone - including you!
